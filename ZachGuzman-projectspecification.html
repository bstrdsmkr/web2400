Version history

This is a living document intended to serve as a reference and guideline for those involved in the production phase of the project. Changes and revisions will be made as the full scope of the project becomes clearer and as the details of the project begin to take form over the course of the production phase. The Version Control section will be automatically updated with every change made to this document and commited to the project’s git repository. Project Objectives

Objective Statement

This project shall include 3 pages redesigned from the source page to be used as a foundation and example for the future work of upgrading the entire site.

Scope

The project scope shall be expressly limited to the items listed below:

  • Design and prototyping of 3 deliverable pages sourced from the original site
  • Development of related design composites
  • Fundamental project management and scheduling
  • Technical consultation
  • Site marketing strategy

Success Criteria

Requirements

  • Site shall be free from typograpical errors
  • Site shall be standards compliant
  • Site shall be free from broken links

Intangible Goals

  • Site shall encourage both users and artists to form a community around the site
  • Site shall present a clean aesthetic while maintaining the brand identity already built by SOAPIFF
  • Site shall scale appropriately for mobile viewing
  • Site shall present relevant information in an easily accessible and straight-forward manner

Confirmation fo Success

A set of automated tests shall be developed to provide confirmation of requirements. Any goals which cannot be verified automatically shall be verified manually and an affidavit or completion shall be included with the testing confirmation

Functional Specification

Navigation

The navigation for the site will consist of a standard top anchored header which presents the SOAPIFF branding along with a link to each of the top level nodes of the site map. The SOAPIFF branding shall serve as the link back to the “Home” page which describes the purpose of the site and what the site offers.

User Expierence

The site will employ responsive design to allow users to easily navigate and explore the site whether from mobile phones, tablets, desktops, or wide screen devices. On smaller screens, unnecessary items will be hidden, images will be scaled to an appropriate size, and text and other design elements will reflow to a more comfortable layout. On smaller devices, the navigation bar will collapse show only the SOAPIFF branding and a menu button which will contain the other navigation options. Marketing Initiatives

Primary Objective

The primary mode of advertisement for SOAPIFF will be through word-of-mouth and community building. While the site will be redesigned and structured to focus on this goal, its achievement will still require some additional effort on the part of the SOAPIFF staff in the form of community outreach and closer interaction with the artists in the community.

Optional Recommendations

While not necessary for the success of the project, the SOAPIFF staff may jumpstart the community building process by investing in advertising in both print and digital media to raise public awareness of the SOAPIFF mission in coordination with the launch of the new site.

Testing Plan

Manual Testing

In addition to the ongoing testing done through development and quality assurance, the Project Manager shall perform usability and specification compliance testing before submission of the project and deliver and affidavit of completion. This testing shall include checking for propper rendering in all supported browsers, as well as the 5 most popular screen sizes and resolution at the time of delivery.

Automated Testing

In addition to the manual testing described in the previous section, a set of automated tests shall be developed in order to ensure efficiency in development of the site. These tests shall be ran against the site under development after each code check in and further development will be suspended until all tests are passed. While these tests may be delivered to SOAPIFF if desired, the expertise and training necessary to employ and maintain these tests is not covered within the scope of this project.
